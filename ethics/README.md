# `ethics/` — Ethics and privacy documentation

This directory contains the ethics documentation of the Torddis study.

## Files

| File | Language | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| `informed-consent-template-ES.md` | Spanish | Blank template of the informed consent form for guardians. **This is the canonical version**, the one actually administered to the participants. |
| `informed-consent-template-EN.md` | English | English translation of the informed consent template, provided for international readers and reviewers. |
| `minor-assent-template-ES.md` | Spanish | Blank template of the child assent form (read-aloud, age-adapted). **Canonical version**. |
| `minor-assent-template-EN.md` | English | English translation of the child assent template. |
| `data-management-plan.md` | English | Data Management Plan aligned with FAIR principles and ISO/IEC 27001:2022. |
| `privacy-impact-assessment.md` | English | Privacy Impact Assessment: 8 risks identified and mitigated. |

## Canonical version and translations

Because the pilot study was conducted with Spanish-speaking participants, the **Spanish** versions of the consent and assent templates are the canonical documents — those are what the guardians and children actually read and signed. The English versions are translations provided for international readers, journal reviewers and future researchers who may wish to replicate the study in English-speaking contexts.

For any subsequent language, please add a translation with the naming convention `<document>-<ISO 639-1 code>.md` (for example, `informed-consent-template-PT.md` for Portuguese).

## Privacy

No completed consent or assent form is included in this repository. The signed originals are kept under lock and key at the Faculty of Engineering Sciences of the Universidad Técnica Estatal de Quevedo (UTEQ) and are accessible only to authorised researchers and, upon reasoned request, to the Research Committee.
