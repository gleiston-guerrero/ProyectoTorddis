# Child assent — age-adapted template (English translation)

> **Important note.** This is a blank template. The Spanish version (`minor-assent-template-ES.md`) is the canonical document that was actually used with the participating Spanish-speaking children. Completed forms are not, and must not be, in the public repository.

## For the child (read aloud)

Hi! We are a group of people doing a study to invent a tiny camera that sits on your table while you do your homework. It is called **Torddis**.

The camera checks whether you are paying attention, whether you are getting sleepy, or whether you are playing with something that is not your homework. If any of that happens, the camera lets a grown-up know on their phone, so they can help you keep going.

We are **not** going to save your face or your voice anywhere. The camera only pays attention to whether you are focused or not.

We will be with you while it works, for about 30 minutes. When we finish, we will ask you what you thought. Anything you tell us will help us make Torddis better.

**You can say no** if you don't want to take part. You can also say **"stop"** at any time, and we will stop. Nothing bad will happen. We won't be upset.

If you say yes, please tell us if you agree:

**[ ]  Yes, I want to take part.**    **[ ]  No, I don't want to take part.**
