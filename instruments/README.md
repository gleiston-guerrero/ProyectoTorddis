# `instruments/` — Data-collection instruments

This directory contains the four instruments used to collect the empirical data reported in the manuscript. All instruments are released under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC-BY 4.0) licence.

## Files

| File | Language | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| `SUS-questionnaire-EN.md` | English | Original 10-item System Usability Scale (SUS) by Brooke (1996). |
| `SUS-questionnaire-ES.md` | Spanish | Version of the SUS administered to the 12 participating guardians. |
| `demographic-survey-EN.md` | English | Demographic survey (English translation). |
| `demographic-survey-ES.md` | Spanish | Demographic survey collected before the pilot session (tutor age, gender, education, occupation, area of residence, internet type, supervision time, child's age). |
| `effectiveness-likert-EN.md` | English | Effectiveness Likert questionnaire (English translation). |
| `effectiveness-likert-ES.md` | Spanish | Seven-item Likert questionnaire designed *ad hoc* to assess the effectiveness of Torddis (Statements 1--7). |
| `open-ended-questions-EN.md` | English | Open-ended questions (English translation). |
| `open-ended-questions-ES.md` | Spanish | Eight open-ended questions attached to the SUS to elicit qualitative feedback on strengths and areas for improvement. |

## Reuse

You are welcome to translate, adapt or extend these instruments for your own studies. Please cite the manuscript and the Zenodo DOI (see root `../README.md`) when doing so.

## Provenance and translations

The Spanish versions were prepared by the research team and reviewed by an education professional prior to administration. No formal back-translation or cross-cultural validation of the *ad hoc* effectiveness questionnaire was performed within the scope of this pilot study.
