# Open-ended questions — English version

Free-text questions attached to the SUS questionnaire to elicit qualitative feedback on the strengths of the Torddis system and its areas for improvement.

## Questions

1. Which aspects of the Torddis system did you find most useful during the supervision session?
2. Which aspects of the system did you find difficult or non-intuitive?
3. Do you think the student behaved differently knowing that Torddis was observing him/her? In what way?
4. Did you notice any moment in which the device failed to correctly recognise the student, his/her facial expression, drowsiness state or any object? Please describe the situation.
5. What changes would you suggest to the mobile application to make it easier to use?
6. What changes would you suggest to the IoT device (shape, size, colours, sound) so that it is better accepted by the child?
7. Do you think Torddis complements or replaces your role as a tutor? Please explain.
8. Would you recommend Torddis to other families? Why or why not?

## Redaction and pseudonymisation protocol

Before depositing the responses in the repository, the following types of identifiable information are replaced:

- Proper names of people → `[NAME]`
- Geographical names finer than the province → `[PLACE]`
- School names → `[SCHOOL]`
- Workplace names → `[WORKPLACE]`
- Addresses, phone numbers or any contact detail → `[CONTACT]`

This protocol is described in more detail in `../ethics/data-management-plan.md`.
