# Demographic survey — English version

Demographic data collected from each guardian before the pilot session. All fields are answered as closed options or ranges and are pseudonymised before being deposited in the repository (see `../data/data-dictionary.md`).

## Guardian data

| Field | Format | Categories |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Age | 5-year range | 25–29, 30–34, 35–39, 40–44, 45–49, 50–54, 55–59, 60–65 |
| Gender | single choice | Female, Male, Other / Prefer not to say |
| Highest completed education level | single choice | Primary, Secondary, Higher (technical), Higher (university), Postgraduate |
| Main occupation | single choice | Paid work, Unpaid domestic work, Student, Retired |
| Area of residence | single choice | Urban, Rural |
| Domestic internet connection type | single choice | Optical fibre, ADSL, Mobile data, Mixed |
| Daily time spent supervising the child's schoolwork | hours | Decimal value 0.5 to 4 h |

## Monitored student data

| Field | Format | Categories |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Age | completed years | 7 to 12 |
| School level | single choice | Primary (grades 1–6), Lower secondary (grades 7–8) |

## Administration notes

- The survey was administered on paper at the beginning of the session.
- No names, surnames, identification documents, addresses, phone numbers or e-mail addresses were requested.
- Data were transcribed into the file `likert-effectiveness.csv` (see `../data/raw/`) with pseudonymous identifiers `G01`…`G12`.
